Art and Existentialism PDF (unpublished essay)

Synopsis:
I wrote an aesthetic component for existential theory that understood consumption of art as a respite for bad faith, and production of art as a respite for nausea. In this essay, art is taken to mean a cultural object such as a performance, painting, or novel. More generally, my intent was to: provide a understanding of art that could be used in identifying Western and non-Western forms of art; account for how genres are created and recognized; write a theory that integrated notions of repression (bad faith as lying-to-yourself) so that it would be compatible with writing in sociology, economics, political science, etc, that draws on notions of ideology; understand art as serving a practical purpose--respite--so that the theory is conducive to quantitative analysis.
